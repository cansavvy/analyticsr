## Privacy Policy  

Last updated: February 13, 2024

By using metricminer you will be interacting with other web services' Application Programming Interfaces (APIs).
The developers of metricminer at no time have any access to any data you collect with metricminer.
We ask that for your own safety you collect and store your own credentials and data with care and do not share your credentials with others. That includes not pushing to GitHub your credentials or user information you've collected with metricminer.
Keep all credentials and user data private.
