  • [Training: Cluster 101 Evaluation] Another round of training evaluation of our Cluster 101 course that is a collaboration between Scientific Computing and the DaSL Training Crew is ongoing. If you’d like to join (even if you know how to use our cluster already), you can do so any time before 1/13 and learn/provide your feedback join us over in #ask-dasl The window for this round of feedback with active support from DaSL/SciComp is 2 weeks (then we’ll be busy integrating your input into the class!) Your feedback will help many future newbies get introduced to and started working on our cluster! There are two links for this class (same content though!). You can take it as a Bookdown course: https://hutchdatascience.org/FH_Cluster_Guide/ or, if you’d like to get the certification, as a (free) Leanpub course: https://leanpub.com/courses/fredhutch/fredhutchcluster101.
