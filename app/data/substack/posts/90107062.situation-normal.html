Welcome to the seventh ever issue of Monday Morning Data Science from the Fred Hutch Data Science Laboratory. Fresh from the Lab

  • [Event: Data House Calls] Wednesday 12/14 at 10am we’ll have our weekly Data House Calls drop in hour in Arnold M1-B867 (and on Teams). Drop by to talk about needed support for data challenges, coding challenges, computing questions, data management, etc.  If the DaSL team can’t help you, they will be able to ping several other folks with different skill sets (including SciComp, the DaSL training group, the Hutch Data Core, etc) and get the right connections for you.

  • [Blog Post: A Brief Introduction to the Tidyverse] Have you ever wanted to just skim the Tidyverse, the uber-popular domain specific language for data frame operations and analysis in R? Well this blog post from our archives was written just for you!
