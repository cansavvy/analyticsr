Fresh from the Lab

  • [Event: Data House Calls] Wednesday 3/1 at 10am we’ll have our weekly Data House Calls drop in hour on Teams and in Amy's office: M1-B867. Drop by to talk about needed support for data challenges, coding challenges, computing questions, data management, etc.  If the DaSL team can’t help you, they will be able to ping several other folks with different skill sets (including SciComp, the DaSL training group, the Hutch Data Core, etc) and get the right connections for you.

  • [Event: Effective Computing Drop in Hour] Wednesday 3/1 at 2pm the Effective Computing user group will meet again (they meet every other week) in room M1-B406 and here on Teams. The focus is on learning about how to scale up your computing work from your laptop to whatever scale meets your needs. This group is hosted by the Fred Hutch DaSL in collaboration with the Hutch Data Core and Scientific Computing.
